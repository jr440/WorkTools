Photo Guide (for real product shots)

  • Daylight near a window; turn off ceiling lights.
  • Neutral background (white card or wood table).
  • One 45° angle shot + a macro detail.
  • Remove stringing; wipe surfaces with isopropyl if safe.
  • Tap to focus; lower exposure a touch.
